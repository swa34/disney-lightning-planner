Disney World Planning Tips

Lightning Lane Strategy

  • Book your first Lightning Lane selections at 7:00 AM on your eligible booking day
  • After using your first Lightning Lane, immediately book your next one in the app
  • Use Early Theme Park Entry (resort guests) to experience popular attractions before utilizing Lightning Lane later
  • Consider Single Passes for the most popular attractions like Rise of the Resistance and Tron

Dining Recommendations

  • Book dining reservations up to 60 days in advance for the best availability
  • Consider eating at off-peak hours (before 11:30 AM or after 2:00 PM for lunch)
  • Use mobile ordering for quick service locations to save time
  • Check the app for same-day cancellations when looking for hard-to-get reservations
